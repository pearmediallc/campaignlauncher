Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to FB Campaign Launcher ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Facebook campaign management application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

When you use FB Campaign Launcher, we collect the following types of information:

2.1 Information from Facebook

  • Facebook User ID: Your unique Facebook identifier
  • Profile Information: Name and email address
  • Business Assets: Ad accounts, Facebook Pages, and Pixels you manage
  • Campaign Data: Information about campaigns you create and manage
  • Access Tokens: Encrypted tokens to access Facebook's API on your behalf
  • Permissions: The Facebook permissions you grant to our application

2.2 Information You Provide

  • Account registration information (name, email, password)
  • Campaign configurations and settings
  • Media files uploaded for campaigns
  • Business preferences and settings

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage Facebook advertising campaigns on your behalf
  • To display your ad accounts, pages, and campaign performance metrics
  • To authenticate and authorize your access to Facebook's advertising platform
  • To save your campaign preferences and templates
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To improve our service and user experience
  • To ensure compliance with Facebook's policies and terms

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • Encryption: All Facebook access tokens are encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored in secure, password-protected databases
  • HTTPS: All data transmission occurs over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel have access to user data
  • Token Expiry: Access tokens automatically expire after 60 days

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Facebook: To execute campaign operations through Facebook's API
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to respond to legal process
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data from our systems
  • Revoke Permissions: Revoke Facebook permissions at any time through Facebook's settings
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Opt-out of certain data processing activities

7. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time. When you request data deletion:

  • We will delete your Facebook access tokens immediately
  • Your campaign data and settings will be permanently removed
  • Any stored media files will be deleted
  • You will receive a confirmation code for your records

To request data deletion, you can either:

  • Use the data deletion option in your account settings
  • Contact us directly at the email provided below
  • Request deletion through Facebook's app settings

8. Facebook's Policies

Our use of information received from Facebook APIs adheres to Facebook's Platform Policies and Facebook's Data Policy. We only request permissions necessary for the functionality of our service and handle all data in accordance with Facebook's requirements.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. These cookies are necessary for the application to function properly. We do not use tracking cookies or share cookie data with third parties.
