1. Introduction

Campaign Launcher ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Facebook advertising campaign management platform.

Important: This policy complies with Facebook Platform Terms and Meta's Business Tools Terms. By using Campaign Launcher, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number when you register
  • Campaign Data: Ad campaign names, budgets, targeting preferences, ad creative content
  • Business Information: Company name, billing information, payment methods
  • Support Communications: Messages sent through our support system

2.2 Facebook Platform Data

When you connect your Facebook account, we access the following data through Facebook's API:

pages_show_list Permission:

  • List of Facebook Pages you manage
  • Page names, IDs, and access tokens
  • Page admin status verification

pages_manage_ads Permission:

  • Create, edit, pause, and delete ad campaigns for your Pages
  • Access campaign performance metrics and analytics
  • Manage ad budgets, schedules, and targeting settings
  • Upload and manage ad creative assets

ads_management Permission:

  • Full access to create and manage Facebook ad campaigns
  • Ad account information and payment methods
  • Campaign, ad set, and ad performance data
  • Custom audiences and lookalike audiences
  • Facebook Pixel data and conversion tracking

ads_read Permission:

  • Read ad performance metrics (impressions, clicks, conversions)
  • Access campaign analytics for custom dashboards
  • Generate performance reports and insights
  • Cost and budget utilization data

business_management Permission:

  • Access to Business Manager accounts
  • Manage multiple ad accounts under Business Manager
  • Asset sharing between business entities
  • Team member permissions and access control

pages_read_engagement Permission:

  • Page post engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares)
  • Organic reach and impression data
  • Audience demographics from Page insights
  • Content performance analytics

leads_retrieval Permission:

  • Lead form submissions from Facebook Lead Ads
  • Contact information of users who submitted lead forms
  • Custom form field responses
  • Lead quality and conversion data

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session data, preferences, and analytics
  • Log Data: Access logs, error reports, performance metrics

5. Data Security and Protection

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Access Controls: Role-based permissions and authentication

  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing

5.2 Data Retention

  • Lead Data: Retained as long as your account is active
